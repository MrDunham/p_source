Simple Terms to Align us all

Sharing in the upside highly motivates Prebacked for your success

For pre-revenue teams (sub $100k revenue per year) we'll typically ask for 5% in common stock. If you've built a product and found traction (more than $100k / year), our ask is closer to 3% and as options.

We're entrepreneurs too, so we built in protection for you

We place all equity under a two year vesting plan, accelerated by your acquision of a paid pilot. If we don't produce enough value, you can kindly ask us to stop vesting and move on. Sometimes it's nice to know someone's thinking of your best interests.
