Hardware & Equipment

Contestants are responsible for supplying the equipment needed to build their projects. Recommended hardware includes, though isn't limited to: laptops, mobile devices, e-readers, desktops and benign robots.

General Restrictions

The following are prohibited from use in the competition:

  • - Medicines or controlled substances
  • - Radioactive materials
  • - Untested, unstable, or otherwise unproven "home brew" hardware
  • - Laser devices designated Class II or higher
  • - Surgical Instruments intended for incising, scoring, or otherwise cutting into organic tissue

Homebrew Hardware

We allow the use of home made hardware and software products in the competition on the condition that they are fully functional and proven safe for use in public environment. You may not bring incomplete, unstable, or unproven products to be finished or tested during the competition.
