What is Prebacked?

Prebacked is a series of hackathons that reverses the traditional way startups get funded. Instead of startup teams pitching ideas to investors, we bring together a group of investors and experts to pitch fundable ideas to startup teams.

What is Prebacked:Health?

Prebacked:Health is our healthcare focused hackathon. A prominent group of health IT investors and industry experts will pitch problems they'd love to fund. Startup teams will have an opportunity to build a solution and immediately showcase it to investors.
