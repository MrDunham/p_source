    Judging criteria

    Submissions are judged by the following four criteria, which are equally weighted.

    Team strength and background

    How strong are the individual team members?
    How well do your abilities complement each other?

    Technical feasibility & implementation costs

    How much effort is needed until our partner can apply your idea?
    How much would the development & integration cost?

    Functional fit

    How well does your product solve the challenge?
    How could our partner benefit from your product?

    Wow factor & innovativeness

    How innovative is your idea / product?
    How could your team / idea impress our partner?
